Doctoral and Post Doctoral Research (Oct' 1999 to Feb' 2007)

Conducted several experiments in Fluvial Mechanics Laboratory of ISI Kolkata to study open channel turbulent flow laden with sediments. Special emphasis was given on studying streamwise velocity distribution vertically, concentration and grain-size distribution over heterogeneous sediment beds.



Research work after joining IIT (Feb' 2007 onwards)

We are developing mathematical models on vertical distribution of fluid velocity, partiical concentration in suspension and grain size distribution. All the models developed are compared with existing experimental data in literature. Together with this, we are developing analytical expressions on some parameters which are essentialy needed to solve the governing equations to determine the turbulent features previously mentioned. Apart from this deterministic approach, from 2015, we have started studying hydraulics in open channel through probabilistic approach. Entropy theory and principle of maximum entropy are being applied to develop models on velocity and concentration in an open channel flow.
